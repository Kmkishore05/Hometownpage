Welcome To Salem

Explore Salem, a vibrant city known for its rich heritage, steel industry, lush mango orchards, and scenic hill views.

Tourist Spots

Mettur Dam

Mettur Dam, one of the largest in India, is a vital water source built across the Kaveri River, offering scenic views.

Poolampatti

Poolampatti is a serene riverside village known for its natural beauty and ferry services across the Kaveri.

Yercard

Yercaud, a hill station in the Shevaroy Hills, is famous for its cool climate, lush coffee plantations, and stunning viewpoints

1008 Lingam Kovil

The 1008 Lingam Temple in Salem is a sacred site with thousands of Shiva lingams, symbolizing devotion and spirituality

Sankagiri Fort

Sankagiri Fort, a historic hill fort, played a key role during the Tipu Sultan and British eras, offering panoramic views.

Oothumalai Hills

Oothumalai Hills is a spiritual spot with ancient temples and breathtaking vistas, attracting devotees and nature lovers alike.
